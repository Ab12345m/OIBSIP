Tribute to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, known as the "Missile Man of India", was a renowned scientist and the 11th President of India. His contributions to India's space and missile programs are legendary. He was not only a brilliant scientist but also an inspiring teacher, writer, and a true patriot who dreamed of a developed India.

His life journey from a small town boy to becoming the President of the world's largest democracy is a true source of inspiration for millions. His famous books like "Wings of Fire", "Ignited Minds", and "India 2020" continue to ignite young minds.

Achievements:

  • Played a major role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Awarded Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor.
  • Worked with ISRO and DRDO in leading positions.
  • Served as the 11th President of India (2002-2007).
